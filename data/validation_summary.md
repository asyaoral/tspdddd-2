## Interpretation

HGA-SHC is the proposed hybrid method. It adds stochastic hill climbing as a local improvement phase after the evolutionary search operations. Therefore, it may require more runtime than the simpler baselines, but it is expected to improve solution quality by reducing the final route cost. The standard deviation column is used as a robustness indicator across different random seeds.
